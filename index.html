About Us

Mission

Transform lives by empowering people with the knowledge, strength and confidence to go on, move on and become their best possible selves.

Who we love to help

Day StarZ Group Home provides therapeutic care to boys and girls between the ages of (3) to (17) years old with "high needs" and "higher risk behaviors" in a safe yet comfortable environment. The facility provides care to children/youth/teens affected by emotional and mental illness disorders, adjudicated sex offenders, sexual maladaptive behaviors, high risk, impulsive, highly disruptive behaviors, substance abuse and others diagnosis.

Day StarZ offer structured environment and strives to help each resident meet the developmental, physical, emotional, social, educational, intellectual and spiritual needs of the residents. Day StarZ will assist in locating resources needed for long term growth and will provide the guidance needed to teach residents to live productive lives to their best of their ability.

Prime location

The facility is conveniently located between Chandler and Casa Grande. This prime location makes it easily accessible to the East Valley and Tucson. Maricopa City is also considered one of the safest and tucked away locations in Arizona.

How we help

Day StarZ works with a group of Clinicians (LCSW’s & BHP’) with extensive backgrounds and specialize in specific focus areas and provide treatment through (multiple) "evidence based practices".

Targeted Case Management will ensure each resident receives additional support needed to meet treatment goals and focus on skill building for the life challenges after treatment.
